White Cars (Most Common)
The white car is the most commonly seen car on Google Street View, with a few different incarnations that can be tricky to tell apart. Geodummy

Rwanda - White, black, or grey/brown colors Geometas
Uganda - A boxy white SUV with square side mirrors Geometas
Ecuador - White car equipped with a short and thick ("stubby") antenna Geometas
UAE - Almost always a white Street View car Geometas
Colombia - Generation 4 coverage features white or silver cars Geometas
Senegal - Silver truck cabs Geometas
Guam - White with distinctive visible bars

Black Cars
The black car is the next most common, seen in parts of Europe and South America, and all Jordan coverage. Geodummy

Jordan - Black cars across all coverage
Colombia - Generation 3 Streetview coverage features black, white, or grey cars Geometas
Curaçao - Black bonnet visible simultaneously with bars Geometas
Bangladesh - Features bars and visible beneath the Street View car
Reunion - Black car with visible bars

Red Cars

Ukraine - Ukraine's google car is red Geodummy
Belgium - Only Ukraine and Belgium have coverage using the red car Geodummy
Sri Lanka - White car with a red blur on the right side, only used in Sri Lanka Geodummy
Nigeria - Large pickup truck with the cab and bed often visible, can be white or red Geometas

Silver/Gray Cars

Kyrgyzstan - White car (sometimes appears silver/gray)

Specialized/Unique Vehicles

Kenya - Snorkel on the front of the Google Streetview car Geometas
Ghana - Roofing with black tape Geometas
Russia - A version of the black car with a visible long antenna on the back, also seen in Donetsk, Israel, and Palestine Geodummy
Christmas Island - Silver pickup/ute/truck Geodummy
US Virgin Islands - Bulky ute (pick-up truck) with bars visible and a tray in the rear Geometas
Mongolia - Visible bars along with red side view mirrors
